If you are feeling suicidal or are a danger to yourself and/or others, please seek professional help immediately. Call or text the crisis hotline at 988 or chat at 988lifeline.org .

Alternatively please call 911 for immediate life threatening situations. More info can be found at https://www.mentalhealth.gov/get-help/immediate-help

You are not alone in your very human experience. Please seek professional help and support.
